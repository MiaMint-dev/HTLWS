Dear readers

I'm Mia Mint; yes, it's a pen name, yes, I was inspired by a box of thin mints on my desk. I write, I code, and I... don't know what to write on a page about me. If you really want to know me, then my Instagram stories are the place.

In hopes of making books more accessible and fun, I'll be uploading every chapter of my book here before I officially publish it. Making this site reminded me of reading web novels on platforms like Wattpad and Quotev in middle/high school. I wanted to revive that experience for everyone interested.

To be fully transparent, there is another reason for this site. I was having a hard time meeting my deadlines. My progress was slowing down by the day. I needed deadlines I couldn't ignore. I wanted to have weekly obligations, rather than to churn away for months in total isolation. And I want to present my story progressively.

I hope you can tune in every week with me to meet Rain, Egon, Hael, and Vaughn.
